I B M - Specializations Certificate - Introduction to Data Science

In this Specialization learners developed foundational Data Science skills to prepare them for a career or 
further learning that involves more advanced topics in Data Science. The specialization entailed understanding what is Data Science is and the various kinds of activities that a Data Scientist’s perform. 
It familiarized learners with various open source tools, like Jupyter notebooks, used by Data Scientists. It taught them about methodology involved in tackling data science problems. The specialization also provided 
knowledge of relational database concepts and the use of SQL to query databases. Learners completed hands-on 
labs and projects to apply their newly acquired skills and knowledge
